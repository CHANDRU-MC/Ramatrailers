company profile

We Are Established in 1952 with the name of RAMA TRAILERS.

located at Coimbatore which has been merged into Rama Trailers. Through our vast industry networking relationships we are able to provide our customers with the most appropriate products specific to their application, at competitive prices. With immense knowledge and leading market know-how, we have registered a dominant position of one of the noted manufacturers, suppliersand exporters in the industry. Founded on the tradition of engineering expertise, we are a leading force in the designing and production of bespoke Agricultural Implements, Cultivtor, Disc Harrow, Disc Plough, Disc Ridger, Leveler, M.B.Plough, Mini Tractor Impliments, Poly Disc Harrow, Sub Soiler, Trolly.We are a leading international Agricultural Implements manufacturer and exporter with an aim to be client's preferred supplier for the agriculture implement sector, through delivering the great value based on innovative implements and World Class Manufacturing Capacities. We are committed to fulfilling customer's requirement by dint of top quality, timely service, competitive prices and on-time supply. We strive to attain our business goal through consistent improvement in the quality-control system and manufacturing mechanism and investments in production technology in order that we can become clients' choice and an employer of preference, ethical and responsible dealings. Up-gradation and enhancement of manufacturing technology and techniques have resulted in the development of materials and expertise in the company.
