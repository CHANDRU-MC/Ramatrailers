Who we are

We Are Established in 1952 with the name of Dhir Sons Engineering Works located at Darapur , Urmar, Tanda, Hoshiarpur, Punjab which has been merged into Dhir Sons Engineering Works in year 1952. Through our vast industry networking relationships we are able to provide our customers with the most appropriate products specific to their application, at competitive prices. With immense knowledge and leading market know-how, we have registered a dominant position of one of the noted manufacturers, suppliersand exporters in the industry. Founded on the tradition of engineering expertise, we are a leading force in the designing and production of bespoke Agricultural Implements, Cultivtor, Disc Harrow, Disc Plough, Disc Ridger, Leveler, M.B.Plough, Mini Tractor Impliments, Poly Disc Harrow, Sub Soiler, Trolly.
